Privacy Policy - RISHTA

RISHTA app is promoted by Uttar Pradesh State Rural Livelihood Mission (UPSRLM) an extended schematic arm of Department of Rural Development (DoRD), Government of Uttar Pradesh (GoUP). UPSRLM is mandated to implement DAY National Rural Livelihood Mission, of Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD), Government of India (GOI).

This app is a unique effort to provide benefits of quick poverty alleviation and government schemes supported by Uttar Pradesh State Rural Livelihood Mission, Rural Development Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh to the members of Self Help Groups (SHGs) in an easy way.Through this app, all the SHGs associated with the Mission will be able to avail the benefits of the schemes themselves through technology-based means without any human intervention; They will not have to run unnecessarily in government offices, their time, energy and money will be saved.
